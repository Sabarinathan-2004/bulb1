About Bulb

    1. An underground resting stage of a plant (as an onion or tulip) consisting of a short stem base bearing one or more buds enclosed in overlapping leaves.
    2. A fleshy structure (as a tuber or corm) resembling a bulb in appearance or function.
    3. A rounded object or part shaped more or less like a bulb.
